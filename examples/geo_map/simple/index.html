Geo Map

An easy way to map data to locations around the world. The .id() key in your data must match the "id" key in the topojson that you import. Click here if you would like to download the topojson we use in this example. The first 2 characters of the IDs we use represent the continent the country is on, and the final 3 characters are the ISO 3-digit country code. For example, the United States is "nausa": "na" for North America and "usa" for the country.
